SERVICES

key attributes

get great reviews

Service at the Ritz is top of the range, as expected at an establishment of this calibre, with staff effortlessly bending backwards to accommodate guests and make them feel at home. There are two restaurants and two bars, as well as dedicated salons for afternoon tea and brunch.

paceful time

We recognize that tranquility and undisturbed rest are crucial for a truly refreshing and rejuvenating experience.

strategic location

The Ritz is tethered to the majestic Place Vendôme, with its canted corners that give it an octagonal aspect, surrounded by grand townhouse apartments and haute jewellery showrooms slotted in at the bottom.

room reviews

Following its extensive renovation in 2016, the Ritz now has 142 rooms split into eight room categories, as well as grand suites named after some of the hotel's most iconic guests, such as Coco Chanel and the Duke and Duchess of Windsor. All the rooms come in the hotel's classic opulent style.

payment options
